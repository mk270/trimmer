I have therefore in the following Discourse endeavour'd to
explain the Trimmer's Principles and Opinions, and then leave
it to all discerning and impartial Judges, whether he can with
Justice be so Arraign'd, and whether those who deliberately per-
vert a good Name, do not very justly deserve the worst that
can be put upon themselves.
